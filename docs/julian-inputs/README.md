## Provenance and fidelity notes

- The email is dated **Wed, May 27, 2026**, from `guevara.j@foxvalleychurch.org`
  to `tomhalfman22@gmail.com`, subject "Life groups: Systems conversation."
- Julian's **answers** are preserved verbatim; the **questions** were asked by
  text and are not in the source, so question topics shown in the capture are
  inferred and labeled as such.
- The multiplication-plan `.docx` is a flat paragraph list — **no tables, tabs,
  or columns** in the underlying XML. The two timeline headers ("Multiply by
  2026" / "Multiply by sometime in 2027") therefore cannot be unambiguously
  mapped to individual leaders from the source; this is flagged in that file.
- The care-list `.xlsx` contained **only a header row** (an empty template), so
  no member data is reproduced.
- Personal names, member counts, and pastoral notes are reproduced because they
  are in the source Julian supplied. Treat this folder as **admin-sensitive**;
  it carries the same confidentiality expectations as the Shepherd Care module.
