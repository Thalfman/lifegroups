## Provenance and fidelity notes

- The email is dated **Wed, May 27, 2026**, from `guevara.j@foxvalleychurch.org`
  to `tomhalfman22@gmail.com`, subject "Life groups: Systems conversation."
- **Both halves of the Q&A are now captured verbatim.** Questions come from
  `Questions.md` (the list Tom sent by text); answers come from Julian's email
  (`Answers.pdf`). An earlier capture had only the answers and *inferred* the
  question topics — that gap is now closed, which in turn corrected two
  conclusions (see [`FEEDBACK_MAP.md`](./FEEDBACK_MAP.md) §0).
- The multiplication plan is a **flat paragraph list — no tables, tabs, or
  columns.** The two timeline headers ("Multiply by 2026" / "Multiply by
  sometime in 2027") therefore cannot be unambiguously mapped to individual
  leaders from the source; this is flagged in that file.
- The care-list `.xlsx` contained **only a header row** (an empty template,
  range `A1:G1` with a blank `F1` spacer), so no member data is reproduced.
- Personal names, member counts, and pastoral notes are reproduced because they
  are in the source Julian supplied. Treat this folder as **admin-sensitive**;
  it carries the same confidentiality expectations as the Shepherd Care module.
