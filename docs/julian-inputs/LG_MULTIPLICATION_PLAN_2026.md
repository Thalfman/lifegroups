## Multiplication criteria

> \*Note – The following is a helpful list to discern potential groups who are
> ready to multiply. A group does not need to meet each of these criteria.

- 3+ years as a life group
- 1+ year(s) of Co Life Shepherd serving
- 12+ members in the life group
- The need for a LIFE group in a similar stage of life
- Willingness from Shepherd(s) to multiply

## Reading the entries (interpretation)

The source does not define its notation. Best reading:

- `(N)` after a name = current member count.
- A second `(Name)` = a successor/apprentice or over-shepherd associated with
  the group (e.g. `(Tony L.)`, `(Gonzalez)`, `(Marshalls)`). **Ambiguous in the
  source.**
- `(?)` = to be determined / unconfirmed.
- `during the day` / `evening` = the group's meeting time.
